Finalize     
To Finalize the contract and make tokens transferable do the following steps (under investment address)
  • Open your wallet (Parity, MyEtherWallet)
  • Click the contracts tab in your wallet
  • Copy the contract address to the Contract Address
    {{ngPopUp.params.contract.contract_details.eth_contract_crowdsale.address}}
  • Copy MyWill data to ABI/JSON interface
    {{ngPopUp.params.contract.contract_details.eth_contract_crowdsale.abi|json}}
  • Select the FINALIZE function
  • Click send transaction
  • Confirm the steps and press Done button
