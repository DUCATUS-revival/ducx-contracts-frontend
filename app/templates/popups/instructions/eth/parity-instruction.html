Add a contract the Lost key in Parity
  • Open Parity
  • Enter Settings and click the Contracts checkbox.
  • Go to folder "Contracts" and click "+ WATCH"
  • In the "Choose contract type" choose "Custom contracts".
  • In the "Network address" field, enter the Management add-on (your Wallet address)
    {{ngPopUp.params.contract.contract_details.eth_contract.address}}
  • Then click "+ ADD CONTRACT"
  • Now this contract is available to you in the list of contracts and you can transfer funds from it.
