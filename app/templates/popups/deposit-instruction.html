Deposit     
To make a deposit on the contract, follow the instructions.
  • Open your wallet (Parity, MyEtherWallet)
  • Choose Send Ether & Tokens tab in your wallet
  • Insert the following recipient's address:
    {{ngPopUp.params.contract.contract_details.eth_contract.address}}
  • Specify how much ETH you want to send (Amount to Send field)
  • Specify Gas limit 30000
  • Click generate Transaction
  • Confirm the payment by pressing the button "DONE"
