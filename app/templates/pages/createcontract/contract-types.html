Will
Is the first smart contract developed in the framework of the platform and realizing the transfer of the user`s savings to his family or friends, in case of his sudden or death.
Lost key
The smart contract has all the same features as the standart Ethereum wallet and, in addition, redirecting your founds to the backup wallet, if you will lose the privat key.
Deffered payment
A smart contract that provides the ability to automate the transfer of funds or payment on a specified date.
Wedding
Smart contract alloving to have two users a common crypto-currency account with a multisig function and disribtion of fund between the heirs.
