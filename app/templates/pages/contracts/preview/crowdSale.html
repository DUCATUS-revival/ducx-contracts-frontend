If you need to verify contract on Etherscan please contact us by email or follow the instruction:


  • Open etherscan
  • In Contract Code tab choose "Verify And Publish"
  • Enter Contract Name:
    {{selectedContract === 'eth_contract_crowdsale' ? 'TemplateCrowdsale' : 'MainToken'}}
  • Enter Contract Address:
    {{contract.contract_details[selectedContract].address|toCheckSum}}
  • Fill out Compiler version:
    {{contract.contract_details[selectedContract].compiler_version|compilerVersion}}
  • Paste the Solidity Contract code:
    {{contract.contract_details[selectedContract].source_code}}
  • Enter constructor arguments ABI-encoded:
    {{contract.contract_details[selectedContract].constructor_arguments}} 
  • Others fields are not required. Press "Verify And Publish"
